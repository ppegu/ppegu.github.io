Privacy Policy - Gyaan Points

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, or phone number (only if you register or log in).
  • Device ID or a hashed identifier used for offline syncing (e.g., QR login for cashout).

b. Non-Personal Information:

  • Progress data like quizzes completed, GP earned, and time spent.
  • Device type, OS version, anonymized usage analytics.
  • GP (Gyaan Points) balances and activity synced securely with backend.

c. Ad-Related Data:

  • We use Google AdMob to display ads (rewarded & banner).
  • AdMob may collect your device ID for ad personalization. See AdMob Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve educational content and user experience.
  • Track learning progress and unlocks.
  • Provide GP rewards and redemptions.
  • Show relevant advertisements.
  • Prevent abuse or misuse of the platform.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data. We may share with:

  • Google AdMob for advertisement purposes.
  • Authorities when required by law.

4. GP to Cashout

We operate a separate cashout app that allows syncing your GP through QR code or login ID. No banking data is collected. Cashouts are processed manually or via trusted UPI apps.

5. Data Retention

We only store data as long as it is required for app functionality and rewards. You can request data deletion anytime via email.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Choices

  • Provide only the necessary personal info (some features may be limited).
  • Disable ad personalization in your device settings.
  • Email us to remove your account/data.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security to protect your information from misuse or unauthorized access.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The most recent version will always be published here.
